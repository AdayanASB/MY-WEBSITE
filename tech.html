ROBOT

A small robot that can move and has an arm to catch and hold objects. It can be controlled by two people one with a keyboard and one with a joystick or in single "player" mode, just with a keyboard.

It Started as a fun idea in my head, and toghether with one of my best friends, we decided to build it. At it's core is a RaspberyPi, some motors, some servo-motors and an Arduino Uno.
