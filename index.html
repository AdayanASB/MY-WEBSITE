Hey! My name is Baias Andrei Silviu and I am a developer and an artist based in Romania with experience ranging from prototyping robots to playing the guitar!

ABOUT ME

DEVELOPER

My programming journey started back in 5th grade, when I discovered that computers have more to them than just games. Ever since, I was always on the quest to expand my knowledge because I felt in love with the IT industry. Right now, i think i tried basically everything from html and css, game dev and app develop all the way to prototyping and building robots. I am driven by curiosity and a burning passion to get better everyday. I am a great team player and I will not stop untill I succeed!
